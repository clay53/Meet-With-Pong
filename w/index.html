Welcome to Meet With Pong!

With Meet With Pong, you will be given the opportunity to meet new people while playing a friendly game of pong! Just enter some basic information about yourself and hit either "Join Live" to play with real random players or hit About Me to play a game against a bot and learn about me, the creator of this experience. The game ends when both players reveal everything about the other player.
